Common Topics

Order & Shipping

Questions about your preorder, delivery status, or shipping timelines? Include your order number in your email for faster resolution. Orders are typically dispatched within 5 business days.

Returns & Refunds

Under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (India), you may return a defective or damaged device within 30 days for a full refund or replacement. Non-defective returns are accepted within 7 days if the device is in original, unused condition. Contact us to initiate a return.

Device & App

Having trouble pairing your NFC device, verifying it in the app, or setting up app blocking? Our team can help troubleshoot. Please include your iOS version and any error messages you see.

Warranty

The Tyme Boxed NFC device is covered by a 6-month warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. To claim warranty service, contact us with your order details and a description of the issue.
